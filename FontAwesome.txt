FontAwesome Icon Conversion:

1. Open icon page, hover over icon and screenshot it into paint.net
2. Remove hand and 7x label, use fill tool to fill with white
3. Increase brightness by 10%
4. Use GrimColorReaper, set to 2.0x tolerance and 0% opacity
5. Use HSL adjustment, S to 0, L to 1
6. Crop to 112x112
7. Select individual components into a separate file, recolor at 75% hardness, 100% tolerance
8. Combine, flatten, save.
